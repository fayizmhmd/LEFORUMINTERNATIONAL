R.V. COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING (RVCE)

R.V. College of Engineering (RVCE), located in Bangalore, India, is a renowned institution known for its excellence in engineering education and research. Established in 1963 by the Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust, RVCE has consistently been ranked among the top engineering colleges in India.

https://www.rvce.edu.in/
Introduction
  • With over five decades of experience in engineering education, RVCE has built a strong legacy of academic excellence, innovation, and industry-relevant research. The college is recognized for producing skilled engineers who excel in their respective fields.
  • RVCE offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering disciplines such as Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Information Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and more. The college also offers programs in Architecture and Management studies.
  • The campus of RVCE is equipped with modern infrastructure and facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, libraries, lecture halls, research centers, sports complexes, and hostels. The college continuously upgrades its facilities to provide students with a conducive learning environment.
  • RVCE boasts a highly qualified and experienced faculty comprising professors, researchers, and industry experts who are dedicated to imparting quality education and fostering research and innovation. The college encourages faculty and students to engage in interdisciplinary research projects and collaborations.
  • RVCE has strong ties with leading industries and organizations, facilitating internships, industrial visits, and collaborative projects for students. The college has an excellent track record of placements, with top-tier companies recruiting students through campus placements.
  • In addition to academics, RVCE emphasizes the holistic development of students by encouraging participation in extracurricular activities, cultural events, technical symposiums, workshops, seminars, and community service initiatives. The college has numerous student clubs and societies that cater to diverse interests and talents.
  • RVCE boasts a vast and influential alumni network comprising accomplished professionals, entrepreneurs, and leaders in various fields. Alumni of the college actively engage with the institution by mentoring students, providing guest lectures, and supporting initiatives for the college's growth and development.

R.V. College of Engineering is located in Bangalore, India.

RVCE offers undergraduate (B.E., B.Tech), postgraduate (M.Tech, MCA, M.Arch), and doctoral (Ph.D.) programs in various engineering disciplines such as Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Information Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and more.

Yes, R.V. College of Engineering is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi.

RVCE is equipped with modern infrastructure and facilities, including lecture halls, laboratories, libraries, research centers, sports complexes, hostels, and cafeteria. The college continuously upgrades its facilities to provide students with a conducive learning environment.

Admission procedures and requirements vary depending on the program and course you are interested in. Detailed information about the admission process, eligibility criteria, entrance exams, and application deadlines is available on the college's official website.

Yes, R.V. College of Engineering provides hostel facilities for both male and female students. Accommodation is available on the college campus, and students can apply for hostel accommodation during the admission process.
