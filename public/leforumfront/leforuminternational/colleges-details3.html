BMS COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING (BMSCE)
  • 5.0 4.3 rating

MS College of Engineering (BMSCE), located in Bangalore, India, is one of the oldest and most prestigious engineering colleges in the country. Established in 1946 by late Sri. B.M. Sreenivasaiah, BMSCE has a rich history of providing quality education and producing skilled engineers.

https://www.bmsce.ac.in/
Introduction
  • With over seven decades of excellence in engineering education, BMSCE has earned a stellar reputation for its commitment to academic rigor, innovation, and research. It is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
  • BMSCE offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering disciplines such as Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Information Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and more. The college also offers programs in Architecture and Management studies.
  • The campus of BMS College of Engineering boasts modern infrastructure and facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, research centers, libraries, auditoriums, sports complexes, and hostels. The college continuously upgrades its infrastructure to meet the evolving needs of students and faculty.
  • BMSCE has a distinguished faculty comprising experienced professors, researchers, and industry experts who are committed to imparting quality education and fostering innovation. The college promotes research and encourages students to engage in projects and interdisciplinary studies.
  • BMS College of Engineering has strong ties with leading industries and organizations, facilitating internships, industrial visits, and collaborative projects for students. The college has an excellent track record of placements, with top-tier companies recruiting students through campus placements.
  • In addition to academics, BMSCE emphasizes the holistic development of students by encouraging participation in extracurricular activities, cultural events, technical symposiums, workshops, seminars, and conferences. The college has numerous student clubs and societies that cater to diverse interests and talents.
  • BMSCE boasts a vast and influential alumni network comprising accomplished professionals, entrepreneurs, and leaders in various fields. Alumni of the college actively engage with the institution by mentoring students, providing guest lectures, and supporting initiatives for the college's growth and development.
