ST.JOHN'S MEDICAL COLLEGE (SJMC)
  • 5.0 4.3 rating

St. John's Medical College (SJMC) is a prestigious medical institution located in Bangalore, India. Established in 1963 by the Catholic Bishops' Conference of India (CBCI), SJMC is affiliated with the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS) and is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI).

https://stjohns.in/
Introduction
  • St. John's Medical College has a rich legacy of over five decades in medical education and healthcare. It is renowned for its commitment to excellence in medical training, research, and patient care. The college has consistently been ranked among the top medical colleges in India.
  • SJMC offers undergraduate and postgraduate medical education programs, including MBBS, MD, MS, DM, and MCh courses. The college also offers diploma and certificate courses in various medical specialties and allied health sciences.
  • SJMC provides comprehensive clinical training to its students through its associated teaching hospital, St. John's Medical College Hospital. The college emphasizes hands-on clinical experience, research, and innovation to prepare students for professional practice and leadership roles in healthcare.
  • The campus of St. John's Medical College is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities, including lecture halls, laboratories, libraries, research centers, simulation labs, and advanced medical equipment. The college continuously upgrades its facilities to meet the evolving needs of medical education and healthcare delivery.
  • SJMC boasts a distinguished faculty comprising experienced medical professionals, researchers, and academicians who are experts in their respective fields. The faculty members are dedicated to imparting quality education, conducting research, and providing compassionate patient care.
  • St. John's Medical College is committed to serving the community and addressing healthcare challenges at the grassroots level. The college actively engages in outreach programs, health camps, and initiatives to promote public health, preventive medicine, and healthcare access in underserved areas.
  • SJMC collaborates with leading international institutions, universities, and healthcare organizations to facilitate exchange programs, research collaborations, and knowledge sharing. These collaborations enrich the educational experience and expose students to global best practices in medicine and healthcare.
